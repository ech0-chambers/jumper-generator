Neckband

  1. Seam the right shoulder.
  2. Re-hang the front neckline from needle 148 left to needle 1 left.
  3. Re-hang the back neckline from needle 1 right to needle 86 right.
  4. The purl side should be facing you. If not, you've seamed the wrong shoulder. Just re-hang with the front panel on the right-hand needles and the back panel on the left-hand needles.
  5. Knit for 32 rows.
  6. Re-hang the first row of the neckband. Bring all needles out to hold position, but do not put the carriage into hold.
  7. Knit one loose row. You may need to hand manipulate the stitches instead of using the carriage.
  8. Cast off, preferably with a chain stitch/crochet cast off.
  9. Seam the other shoulder, including the neckband.
  10. Seam the sleeves to the body.
  11. Seam the side seems and sleeves.
